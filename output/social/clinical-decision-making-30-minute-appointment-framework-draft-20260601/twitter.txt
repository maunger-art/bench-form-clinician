[1/5] Thirty minutes to assess, treat, and progress a complex MSK patient. Here's a framework for making defensible progression decisions when time is the scarcest resource.

---

[2/5] Phase one: 5-7 minutes to establish baseline function using 2-3 reproducible tests. Not comprehensive assessment, just the impairments that will actually drive treatment selection.

---

[3/5] Phase two: target your 15-minute treatment window at what those tests showed. Precision rehab aimed at identified deficits outperforms generic protocols (Longo et al., 2020).

---

[4/5] Phase three: define progression in numbers before the patient leaves. 12 single-leg calf raises to 20 is a 67% improvement both of you can track.

---
