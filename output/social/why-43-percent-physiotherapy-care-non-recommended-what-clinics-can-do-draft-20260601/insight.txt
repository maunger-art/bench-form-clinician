Up to 43% of MSK physiotherapy care doesn't align with current evidence-based guidelines, not because clinicians lack skill, but because decisions in post-surgical rehab, chronic pain management and return-to-sport protocols often rely on subjective judgement rather than objective measurement. This gap shows up operationally too: inconsistent care makes staffing harder, pricing less defensible and outcomes harder to justify to commissioners. Closing it starts with building objective assessment into existing treatment sessions.
