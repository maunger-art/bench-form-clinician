[1/5] 43% of MSK physiotherapy care doesn't follow current evidence-based guidelines. Not a competence problem. A measurement problem. Here's where the gaps cluster and what closes them.

---

[2/5] Post-surgical rehab is the most variable area. After ACL reconstruction, early open kinetic chain exercises can safely speed recovery with proper progression criteria. Many clinicians still avoid them by default.

---

[3/5] Blood flow restriction training has strong evidence for strength and muscle mass gains. Adoption stays inconsistent, often because clinicians lack confidence in pressure levels and progression parameters.

---

[4/5] Chronic pain management shows the same gap. For subacromial bursitis, combined physiotherapy and corticosteroid injection often outperforms either alone, but clinics rarely coordinate with medical colleagues to deliver it.

---
